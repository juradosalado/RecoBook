  • How does Recobook work?

    Recobook is a Dialogflow agent that use Natural Language Processing to understand what user says and process it to data. Recobook catch your reading preferences and throw that information to a Recommendation System with constraint-based recommendation, which calculates a score for every single book in Data Base based in your preferences.

  • How can I use Recobook?

    To use Recobook, you just have you respond our chatbot questions as he suggest you to do. For every parameter that Recobook can scrore for you, it will first ask you for a particular value for that parameter. For example, for genres it will ask you what genres would you want your next read to have. In this type of questions, you can always say you don't mind about this parameter in your book recommendation. However, be sure of always specify what parameter you are not interested in. For example, for genres you could answer "I'm not interested in genres". The, if you are actually interested in that parameter, the chatbot will ask you a relevance number, which must be an integer from 1 to 10. that relevance will be important to add more or less scrore to book that accomplish your parameter preference. At the end of the conversation, Recobook will provide you a link with your recommendation results. I hope you enjoy it!
